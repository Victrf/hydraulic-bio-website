Established in 2014, Attral Global Tech Private Limited ISO 9001:2015 certified company, is a distinguished manufacturer, importer, exporter, and service provider specializing in a diverse range of products. Our offerings include:Benz & Fuso Truck PTOs Hydraulic Cylinders Hydraulic Pumps Tata Truck PTOs PTO Accessories ZF Gearbox PTOs Hidromas Hydraulic Spares & Kits Truck Mounted Cranes Our commitment is to exceed customer expectations by delivering exceptional quality products tailored to their specific needs. We uphold rigorous ethical standards and maintain transparency in all our transactions, fostering strong and lasting relationships with our clients. We extend our sincere gratitude to Mr. Philip Carey for his unwavering support and guidance, which have been instrumental in driving our significant growth and success in the market.

Attral Global Tech Pvt Ltd began as a small startup a decade ago, founded in 2014 by Mr. Philip Carey. Initially established in Bangalore with a modest team, the company has experienced remarkable growth over the past ten years. Today, Attral Global Tech is a private limited company, officially registered in 2022 under MSME regulations, and has built a strong reputation and brand presence in the Asian market With over 20 years of collective experience, Attral Global Tech specializes in the design and research and development of truck hydraulic equipment. Our collaboration with European counterparts enhances our capabilities, allowing us to deliver innovative solutions tailored to the needs of the Asian and African markets. The name "Attral" was derived from an ancient Tamil word meaning "Power, Energy, Potential," reflecting our commitment to harnessing these attributes in our operations. Attral Global Tech Pvt Ltd remains dedicated to upholding its core values and striving for excellence at every level.
